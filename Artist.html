Artist

Seldom A.Huuman

British-Nigerian, Seldom A. Human started making music in high school. Due to her shortcomings not being able to work or go to school she developed her talent for music. Started out rapping but due to her lack of beats she decided production best suit her style. She blends multiple genre to give an upbeat refreshing sound. Her goal is to influence the masses to be differnt and not to be afraid of being differnt because in fact being unique sets one up to have their own sound.

Dino De Leon

Born and raised in south Florida to immigrant parents. Since a young age, Dino was exposed to an eclectic array of music establishing a strong love for the art form and eventual practice, once he joined band in both middle and high school. Dino continued his education in music, studying audio engineering in Miami and London, UK. While in London, Dino worked at the RedBull studio where he was able to work on several projects and continue to learn from masters of the craft. When Dino returned from London he started FATTL, LLC., a multimedia company with the focus of creating and promoting high quality art.
