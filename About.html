FATTL, LLC is a record label made up of creative partners that strive to generate a multimedia, in-house empire that is self-sufficient. A record label that expresses creativity that has everything it needs to produce music, videos, art and fashion.

Our mission is to create high quality art in all avenues of music, film, and fashion making it easily accessible for the masses. FATTL aspires to influence creativity and help people get through the rough patches' life brings forth, with different art forms.

We hope to motivate customers and create inspirations for others to feed upon that’s why the label is named FATTL, an acronym for Feed Art To The Lions. Lions are our listeners, our customers. FATTL is all about change within music and provokes growth within oneself.
